Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

Glow’s Haven LLC (“we,” “us,” or “the Company”) respects your personal privacy and is committed to protecting your data signatures. This operational policy transparency document outlines exactly how we manage and safeguard incoming transmissions across our primary domains, networks, and modular ecosystem hubs (Sea Haven, Wings DAO).

1. Data We Collect

We strictly limit tracking systems to data vectors required to validate transactions, ensure platform stability, and execute sanctuary milestones:

  • Voluntary Profiles: If you elect to register for our newsletters, utilize the contact interface, or participate in localized programs, we collect your stated names, aliases, and direct electronic mail addresses.
  • Web3 Infrastructure Logs: We access public cryptographic ledger address vectors when you cross-verify NFT assets or process custom donations. Note: Glow's Haven LLC never asks for, records, or interfaces with private cryptographic seed keys.
  • Automated Matrix Logs: Standard system metrics (masked IP addresses, base browser layouts) are checked to guarantee web safety, security firewalls, and performance scale.

2. How We Use Your Data

Information processed by our LLC dashboard is strictly limited to the following contexts:

  • To directly clear, track, and verify philanthropic asset goals for Sea Haven.
  • To dispatch strategic updates, architecture logs, and requested whitepapers.
  • To confirm public token ownership to open custom membership tiers inside our strategy vaults.
  • To maintain flawless corporate compliance with our standard statutory obligations as a registered Tennessee LLC.

3. Blockchain Transparency Notice

Public cryptographic ledgers (such as the Solana network) operate on immutable open registries. While real-world physical signatures are not linked directly on this server to public asset accounts, your historic interaction records across the network ledger are permanent, public, and independent of Company control or data removal systems.

4. Third-Party Sharing Rules

We do not sell user data. Information packets are shared only under high-integrity parameters:

  • Charity Audits: Sharing required variables to produce accurate conservation, impact tracking reports, or rescue updates.
  • Traditional Gateways: Processing traditional merchant donations using heavily protected industry-standard clearers (such as Stripe or PayPal).
  • Statutory Orders: Actions required by state or federal judicial processes to protect the safety of veterans or physical sanctuary land infrastructure.

5. Your Rights (Tennessee Information Protection Act - TIPA)

Operating out of Tennessee, we respect and fully honor your digital rights under state regulations:

  • Access: The right to clear verification of whether your records are being handled.
  • Correction: The right to update inaccurate text or system identifiers.
  • Erasure: The right to call for complete local database asset drops. (Note: Data hardcoded to a public blockchain layer cannot be extracted or deleted).

6. Security Engineering

We deploy robust digital firewalls to insulate data pipelines from unauthorized breaches. However, since no network matrix is entirely infallible, we strongly advise our community members to enforce rigorous personal Web3 hygiene practices, hardware vaults, and separate access keys.

Glow’s Haven LLC: Built in Web3. Rooted in Tennessee. Defined by Integrity.
