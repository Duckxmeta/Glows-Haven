ODIN’S PLACE: A SAFE HARBOR IN TENNESSEE

The ultimate goal of Glow’s Haven LLC is the establishment of a physical retreat. Located on several acres of serene Tennessee land, Odin’s Place serves as a private sanctuary for recovery, reflection, and rejuvenation.

The Mission Detail

  • Veteran Peer Support: A dedicated space for those who have served to find kinship and peace away from the noise.
  • Canine Advocacy: Home to our specialized service dog programs, focusing on the unbreakable bond between humans and their companions.
  • Nature Based Healing: Utilizing the natural beauty of the land to provide a soft landing spot for those navigating life’s heaviest transitions.

THE MODEL: VALUE IN, VALUE OUT (VIVO)

The gears of our ecosystem. Every spoke feeds a defined fund with precision and transparency.

WINGS DAO PATH

Proceeds flow into the Land Acquisition Fund to support physical expansion and infrastructure of the haven.

SEA HAVEN PATH

Proceeds flow to Sea Creature Charities and Operational Costs for marine rescue and team development.

Our Commitment to Transparency

At Glow’s Haven, every project serves a specific purpose in our ecosystem. We ensure that your support reaches the intended cause with precision:

  • Wings DAO: Focused on the long term vision of our physical sanctuary. Proceeds from Wings DAO branding and event initiatives are dedicated to the Land Acquisition Fund, securing the space needed for our animals and programs.
  • Sea Haven: Born from the community takeover of the turtle project on Solana, Sea Haven remains dedicated to the deep blue. Funds generated here are strictly allocated to marine life rescue, ocean conservation charities, and the dedicated team members who manage these initiatives as outlined in our project whitepaper.

THE FUTURE: THE ON SITE CREATIVE OFFICE

We are currently in the phase of transitioning our global digital team to a physical administrative hub on site. This office will serve as the Brain Trust for the entire Haven, ensuring that our professional operations are as grounded as our mission.

While our physical headquarters serves as a central hub for strategy and administration, we continue to embrace a hybrid global model. A portion of our international staff will remain as remote workers, ensuring we maintain a diverse, world class team across all time zones.

THE SANCTUARY ROADMAP: FROM DIGITAL SPIRIT TO TENNESSEE SOIL

The Haven is not just a destination; it is a movement in motion. We are currently building the foundation of our physical sanctuary, bridging the gap between digital vision and the earth beneath our feet.
